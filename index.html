Dr Thanh Ngoc Nguyen Research Page

Posted on January 1, 2022 by Dr Thanh Ngoc Nguyen
Information system Health informatics Information infrastructure

Dr. Thanh Ngoc Nguyen has been a lecturer at the School of Science and Technology, RMIT, since 2017. He obtained his Master's and PhD degrees from the University of Oslo, Norway. With extensive experience in software development, he has contributed to the construction of numerous large-scale systems for the Ministry of Health in Vietnam and other countries. His research interests lie in the fields of health informatics, digital transformation, and AI applications in healthcare.
